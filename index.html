Class Comparison

First Class vs Business Class: What's Actually Different?

Naming varies wildly by operator. What Italy calls "Business" is what France calls "First." We decode every class on every major train so you know exactly what you're paying for.

  • First Class typically means 2+1 seating, more legroom, and quieter carriages
  • Executive/Premium class adds single-aisle seats, full meals, and concierge service
  • The biggest quality jump is usually from Standard to First â€” not First to Premium
  • On the Shinkansen, Green Car (first class) is exceptional value; Gran Class is pure indulgence
